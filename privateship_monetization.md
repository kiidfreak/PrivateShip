## üí° Unit Economics

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**SMB/Starter Tier:**
- CAC: $50 (developer-focused content marketing, referrals)
- LTV: $1,200 (12 months retention @ $100/month)
- LTV/CAC: 24:1 ‚úÖ

**Growth Tier:**
- CAC: $200 (direct sales, partnerships)
- LTV: $6,000 (12 months @ $500/month)
- LTV/CAC: 30:1 ‚úÖ

**Enterprise:**
- CAC: $5,000 (sales team, demos, pilots)
- LTV: $60,000 (24+ months @ $2,500/month average)
- LTV/CAC: 12:1 ‚úÖ

### Gross Margin

**Cost per Verification:**
- SMS (Africa's Talking): $0.01
- OCR (SmileID or custom): $0.02
- Compute + storage: $0.005
- **Total COGS:** ~$0.035

**Revenue per Verification:**
- Starter tier: $0.10
- Growth tier: $0.08
- Average: $0.09

**Gross Margin:** 61% (after COGS)

After infrastructure (servers, Redis, PostgreSQL): **~70%** gross margin

## üíé Why This Model Works

### 1. **Infrastructure Play**
- Everyone needs trust, few can build it
- Network effects (more users = better fraud detection)
- High switching costs (integrated into code)

### 2. **Favorable Unit Economics**
- Low COGS (~$0.03/verification)
- High margin (~70%)
- Usage scales with customer success

### 3. **Multiple Expansion Paths**
- Usage growth (more verifications)
- Feature upsells (fraud detection, compliance)
- Geographic expansion (Nigeria, Ghana)
- New use cases (Web3, dating apps, DAOs)

### 4. **Defensive Moat**
- Midnight integration (tech advantage)
- Regulatory compliance (barrier to entry)
- Data network effects (fraud detection improves)
- API lock-in (switching is painful)
